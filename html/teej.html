Teej Festival

Teej Festival or Nepal Women's Festival, is a festival celebrated by women all over Nepal for three days on August. Woman sing and dance to traditional folk songs in this days decking up in red sarees and red tika and bangles. In This festival , married and unmarried women seek the blessings of Goddess Parvati.

It is celebrated in the Hindu month of Bhadrapada on the Tritiya or 3rd day of the Shukla paksha (waning phase of the moon). They also fast one day, this day is a public holiday in Nepal. In this day women wear gold and silver jewelry and dress in their red wedding saris. Then they head in groups to Shiva temples nearby and offer flowers and fruits to Lord Shiva.
