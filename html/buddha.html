Buddha Jayanti Festival

Buddha Jayanti is cebrated on the birth anniversary of Gautam Buddha (the Full Moon Day of May) and is celebrated as the thrice blessed day as all the important events in the life of Lord Buddha â€” His birth in Lumbini, His attainment of Enlightenment in Buddhagaya and entering into Mahaparinirvana in Kusinagar took place on this eventful day. Many Buddhists in this day go to their local temple and some stay there throughout the day and night of the full moon doing good deeds, taking part in chanting and meditation, reflecting on Buddhist teachings, bringing offerings to the temple and sharing food with people.
